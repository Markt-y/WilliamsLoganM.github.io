Budapest Express

You're a famous detective on board a train bound to Budapest. There has been a murder on board and you have 1 hour to solve to murder and stop the train, before the killer can escape into the night. Do you have what it takes to solve the murder and find the killer before you become their next victim? Time is of the essence.
